Privacy Policy

Welcome to ElevateU's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data from participants of our educational platform. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy while providing expert-led education and comprehensive learning resources in financial literacy, academic development, and financial markets.

Data Collection and Use

While using our educational platform, we may collect information about your device, browser, and activity on the website. This may include IP address, browser type, language settings, visit dates and times, as well as data collected through cookies and similar technologies.

Collected data is used to ensure the proper functioning of the platform, improve user experience, and analyze engagement with educational resources. Cookies help remember your preferences and study patterns to enhance expert-led education and comprehensive learning content.

Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device when visiting our website. They help improve navigation, provide personalized educational content, and optimize the learning experience. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, including deleting or blocking them.

Children’s Privacy

Our platform is intended for learners above the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If parents believe their child’s data has been collected, they can contact us to request deletion.

Sharing Data with Third Parties

Personal data is not shared with third parties without your consent, except as required to provide educational services or as required by law. When collaborating with partners, we ensure that data confidentiality and security are maintained.

Data Protection

We apply modern security measures to safeguard your personal information. Encryption and secure storage methods are used to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of data.

Updates to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in practices or regulations. We recommend reviewing this page periodically. Continued use of the platform after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your data, correct, update, or delete it, and to limit or object to its processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Contact Us

For questions regarding our Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please contact: elevate_u@gmail.com.

Thank you for reviewing ElevateU's Privacy Policy as part of our commitment to expert-led education, comprehensive learning, and promoting financial education to democratize knowledge on financial markets.
